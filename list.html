What are Lists in HTML?

In HTML, lists are used to group related items together. They help in presenting information in a structured and readable way. There are three main types of lists:

  • Ordered List (<ol>) → Items are displayed in a specific order (numbers, letters, Roman numerals).
  • Unordered List (<ul>) → Items are displayed with bullets (disc, square, circle).
  • Description List (<dl>) → Defines terms and their descriptions.
