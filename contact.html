Why Have a Contact Page?

A Contact Us page allows visitors to get in touch with the website owner or organization. It can include a form, email address, phone number, or even a map. This page is very important for feedback, queries, and support.

Common Elements in a Contact Page:

  • A simple form to collect user queries.
  • Email address for direct communication.
  • Phone number for urgent contact.
  • Address or Google Maps iframe for location.
